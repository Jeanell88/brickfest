Frequently Asked Questions

How can I purchase a ticket?

Tickets will be available at the door. We accept all major Credit Cards and cash. No Checks will be accepted.

How much will tickets cost?

Children under 2 years of age will be free. Children ages 3-12 will be $10 each. 12 and up will be $15 each.

How do I become a Volunteer?

Please email us here to sign up to become a volunteer and receive more information.

Can I bring my creations built at Brickfest home with me?

No. Creations built at Brickfest must stay at the event.

Where do I report a lost or found item?

Please report any lost articles or return any found items to Guest Services or any event worker. Brickfest is not responsible for theft, damage or loss of personal property.

What are the health and safety protocols put in place during the event?

Hand sanitizing stations situated throughout the event. Social distancing and face coverings are encouraged. Bag checks will be conducted at each entry point before entering the facility.
